Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup flour
  • 2 tablespoons paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 10 chicken wingettes


Directions

Step 1

Preheat oven to 425 degrees.

Step 2

Line a baking sheet with foil. Dot the foil with the butter, there is no need
to melt it ahead of time but you can if you would like.
3 tablespoons butter

Step 3

In a medium sized bowl, combine flour, paprika, garlic powder, and salt
and pepper.
1/3 cup flour,2 tablespoons paprika,1 teaspoon garlic powder,
1 teaspoon black pepper,1 teaspoon salt

Step 4

Coat both sides of each wing fully in the flour mixture and place on the
baking sheet. Make sure the butter pieces are evenly spaced out among
the wings.
10 chicken wingettes

Step 5

Bake wings for 30 minutes.

Step 6

Turn wings over and bake for an additional 15 more minutes or until crispy
and fully cooked.

Step 7

Serve with your favorite dipping sauce and sprinkle with fresh parsley if
desired.
